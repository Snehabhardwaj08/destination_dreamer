Enjoy the Journey

Nestled on the banks of the river Ganga amid the majestic Himalayas, Haridwar and Rishikesh are the two sacred places for devotees. These two destinations attract thousands of travelers every year with its natural essence and beauty. Rishikesh is gifted with the flowing of sacred Ganges and the famous belief amongst Hindu pilgrims is that performing rigor and tendering prayers in the sacred shrines brings the emancipation. On the other hand, Haridwar is a door to the Char Dham which includes Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamunotri, and promises a wonderful experience for tourists. Being of spiritual significance, both the places offer well known attractions such as Har Ki Pauri, Bharat mata Mandir, Sapta Rishi Ashram, Triveni Ghat, Laxman Jhula, Neelkantha Mahadeva and many more. Book Rishikesh Haridwar tour packages from Chandigarh to pay a visit to these places situated in the lap of Mother Nature.
