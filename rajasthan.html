Itinerary

We have carefully planned out each day to give you the best possible experience. From exploring historic landmarks to tasting delicious local cuisine,each day is packed with adventure and excitement. Join us as we take you on a journey through some of the most fascinating destinations in the world.

Welcome to the Pink City, Jaipur! Upon arrival at Jaipur Airport/Railway Station, get transferred to your hotel. Post check-in, relax, and later, get driven for a half-day exploration of the city. Start by admiring the intricate architecture of Hawa Mahal. Then, delve into Jaipur‚Äôs royal history at the stunning City Palace, and continue to Jantar Mantar to see the ancient astronomical instruments. Lastly, pay your respect at the Govind Dev Ji Temple before getting transferred to your hotel.

Today, board your transfer to visit the iconic landmarks of Jaipur. Start with a visit to the Amer Fort and wander through its expansive courtyards. Then, visit Jaigarh Fort to admire the mighty Jaivana cannon, offering insights into the region's past. Continue to the Nahargarh Fort and soak-in the panoramic views of Jaipur's skyline. Also, visit the Jal Mahal, City Palace, and Birla Temple, each offering a unique insight into Jaipur‚Äôs rich cultural tapestry. Post-tour, get driven to your hotel.

In the morning, after check-out, enjoy a scenic drive to the Blue City, Jodhpur. Upon arrival at your hotel, check-in and spend the rest of your day at leisure, where you can relax or explore the city on your own. You can visit the historical Mandore Garden to witness the intricate cenotaphs. You can choose to enjoy a boat ride on the serene waters of Kaylana Lake, a peaceful man-made oasis, or you can enjoy dining at the luxurious Raas Haveli's rooftop restaurant. Later, return to your hotel.

Today, board your transfer for an unforgettable historical tour of Jodhpur. Begin with a visit to the majestic Mehrangarh Fort and soak-in the stunning panoramic views of the blue city below. Also, wander through its ancient corridors, soaking in the rich history and vibrant culture that the fort embodies. Next, admire the serene marble cenotaph adorned with delicate jali work in Jaswant Thada. Later, visit the iconic Ghanta Ghar and soak-in the local culture. Later, get driven to your hotel.

In the morning, after check out, get driven to the Golden City, Jaisalmer. Upon arrival, check-in to your hotel and spend the rest of your day at leisure, where you can relax or explore the city on your own. You can visit the Bada Bagh & witness its striking chhatris, which serve as memorials to the royal family. Also, you can explore the Jaisalmer War Museum & admire the collection of military artifacts, highlighting the region's rich history. After exploring, return to your hotel on your own.

Today, board your transfer and indulge in Jaisalmer's rich heritage and vibrant culture. Begin with a visit to Gadisar Lake and soak-in its surrounding views and vibrant atmosphere. Next, admire the exquisite carvings of Patwon ki Haveli and experience the Rajasthani desert charm at Sam Sand Dunes. Conclude your tour at the Jaisalmer Fort to admire its golden sandstone, offering a glimpse into the region's rich history. Post-tour, get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

In the morning, check out from your hotel In Jaisalmer and get driven to Jodhpur Airport/Railway Station. Upon your arrival, board your transfer back home, This marks the end of your amazing Rajasthan trip!
