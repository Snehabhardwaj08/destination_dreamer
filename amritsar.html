Enjoy the Journey

A journey to the heart of Punjab, Amritsar is among the must visit destinations in the north-west part of India. Our two nights and three days Amritsar Weekend Tour Package takes you to all the famous tourist destinations of this beautiful district. We allow you to get drenched in the hues of spirituality at the Golden Temple; we take you back in the days of freedom struggle at the JallianwalaBagh; we let you have a flashback of the royal yesteryears at the Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum. Moreover, we also take you the Wagah Border, an international borderline between India and Pakistan, wherein you can witness the evening parade. Thus, this weekend tour package to Amritsar brings you closer to Indian history and rich Sikh Culture; thus, book it right away and enjoy an unmatched travel experience.

Itinerary

We have carefully planned out each day to give you the best possible experience. From exploring historic landmarks to tasting delicious local cuisine,each day is packed with adventure and excitement. Join us as we take you on a journey through some of the most fascinating destinations in the world.

Morning after breakfast drive to Amritsar. Arrive and check-in at the hotel. Rest of the day free to explore local market.

After breakfast, proceed to visit Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh and Durgiana Mandir. In the afternoon, proceed to visit Wagah Border to attend the Beating Retreat ceremony held before sunset (Reach the border by 4:30 pm). Return to hotel by evening. Overnight stay at hotel.

After breakfast, drive to Chandigarh.
