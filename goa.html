Enjoy the Journey

Goa is a dream holiday destination for those tourists and vacationers who are keen wanderers and like to soak the beauty of exotic beaches. Whenever one plans to have a wonderful getaway in the midst of golden shimmering beaches, the first place that comes to mind is Goa. This ecstasy region is not only famous for its beaches but also for the elegant hills and cliffs and the old monuments that make this place heavenly and truly delightful. From relaxing on the beachside shacks while the sun gently splashes its golden lights on the water to witnessing beautifully built age-old cathedrals, this former Portuguese territory is one of the most happening places in India. Being the party destination that it is, Goa has all to make sure that you have a memorable time amidst its beautiful beaches.

Itinerary

We have carefully planned out each day to give you the best possible experience.From exploring historic landmarks to tasting delicious local cuisine,each day is packed with adventure and excitement.Join us as we take you on a journey through some of the most fascinating destinations in the world.

  • Meet and get greeted by our official, who will ensure a smooth transfer to your pre-booked hotel.
  • Complete the check-in formalities and rest for some time.
  • Enjoy the first day of your Goa trip leisurely, go out exploring the surroundings or the beaches nearby while having a great time with your loved ones.
  • Return back to your hotel for an overnight stay in Goa.

  • Wake up in the morning and have a scrumptious breakfast.
  • You need to head to the pickup point to board the coach that will take you for a full day sightseeing of North Goa.
  • Start off by visiting the famous Aguada Fort, a 17th-century Portuguese fort looking out at the confluence of Mandovi River and the Arabian Sea.
  • Stroll through the remains of the fort standing tall along the Goan coastline and watch the Arabian sea meeting the horizon.
  • Later, visit Sinquerim Beach which is located just below the Aguada Fort.
  • Move Northward towards the Candolim beach that is a gateway to the other more famous beaches.
  • Spend the evening at the 'most photographed beach in Goa' the Vagator beach, a beautiful crescent-shaped beach crowned by the cliff of Chapora Fort on one side and the Chapora river basin on the other.
  • Return back to the hotel for your overnight stay in Goa.

  • Wake up in the morning, have a delicious breakfast, and get ready for a tour to South Goa.
  • You need to reach the pickup point to board the coach that will take you through the streets of South Goa.
  • Begin your day by visiting the Shri Manguesh temple that is dedicated to Bhagavan Manguesh, an incarnation of Shiva and is worshipped here as Shivlinga.
  • Later, visit the old parts of Goa that house some of the oldest churches in the country.
  • In the evening, you can go Shopping at the Panjim flea market and collect souvenirs for your loved one
  • Return back to the hotel for your overnight stay in Goa.
