Shimla Package

Himachal Pradesh

Duration

6 Days

Prices

INR 23,900

Enjoy the Adventure

Experience the charm of Himachal Pradesh with this Shimla tour, covering scenic hill landscapes, colonial architecture, and adventure activities. Ideal for families, couples, and nature lovers seeking a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration.

Inclusions/Exclusions

To help you plan your trip,we have put together a list of what's included and what's not included in your tour package. this will give you a clear understanding of what to expect and help you make any necessary arrangement before your journey begins.

  • Pickup drop services from Chandigarh
  • Private Dzire/Etios Cab for sightseen
  • 05 Nights accommodation on twin sharing basis as per itinerary
  • Meals as per hotel plan (05 Breakfast + 05 Dinner)
  • All transfers and sightseeing as per the itinerary
  • Road taxes, parking fees, fuel charges, interstate taxes
  • All personal expenses like tips, laundry, telephone calls/fax, alcoholic beverages, camera/video camera fees at monuments, medical expenses, airport departure tax etc.
  • Anything not mentioned under Package Inclusions.
  • Entrance fees at all monuments and sightseeing spots
  • Airfare if not mentioned in the itinerary

Itinerary

We have carefully planned out each day to give you the best possible experience. From exploring historic landmarks to tasting delicious local cuisine,each day is packed with adventure and excitement. Join us as we take you on a journey through some of the most fascinating destinations in the world.

Pick up from Chandigarh Railway Station or Airport and drive to Shimla. Enjoy scenic views of the Shivalik Hills on the way. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel. In the evening, explore Mall Road, The Ridge, and Lakkar Bazaar on your own. Overnight stay in Shimla.

After breakfast, proceed for full-day sightseeing. Visit Kufri and Jakhoo Temple. Later explore Christ Church and Scandal Point. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Shimla.

After breakfast, check out and drive to Manali. En route enjoy beautiful sights like Pandoh Dam, Beas River, and Kullu Valley. Optional stop at Kullu for river rafting or shopping. On arrival in Manali, check in to the hotel. Overnight stay in Manali.

After breakfast, visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Temple and Hot Water Springs, Tibetan Monastery, Club House, Van Vihar, and Manu Temple. In the evening, enjoy shopping and leisure time at Mall Road. Overnight stay in Manali.

After breakfast, go for an excursion to Solang Valley, famous for snow and adventure activities (at own cost). Continue towards Atal Tunnel and Sissu village, subject to weather and road conditions. Rohtang Pass trip can be arranged at extra cost as per NGT guidelines. Return to Shimla. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Breakfast at hotel, check out the hotel. Journey start towards you starting point Chandigarh. With lots of unforgettable memories here your tour End.
